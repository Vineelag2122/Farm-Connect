Soil Analysis

About Soil Types

Black Soil

Rich in calcium, potassium, and magnesium. Excellent for cotton and wheat cultivation.

Red Soil

Rich in iron but poor in nitrogen. Good for groundnut and potato farming.

Alluvial Soil

Very fertile and rich in humus. Perfect for rice and wheat cultivation.

Laterite Soil

Poor in fertility but good drainage. Suitable for tea and coffee plantations.

Loamy Soil

Perfect balance of minerals and drainage. Suitable for most crops.

Soil Care Tips

  • Regular soil testing to monitor nutrient levels
  • Maintain proper soil pH for optimal nutrient absorption
  • Practice crop rotation to prevent soil depletion
  • Add organic matter to improve soil structure
